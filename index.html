Notice the amount of trees in the background, that seems to be the main difference. Not much else seems different.

There is a jump in quality going from low to medium but it seems to stay pretty much the same throughout medium-high-ultra. Difference between medium and ultra is hard to notice, yet medium gives 6 FPS more. When in motion, on ultra there are some more animations on flags and other decorations (nothing that you notice while driving).

There is a significant hit to the framerate going from low to medium, high and ultra.
